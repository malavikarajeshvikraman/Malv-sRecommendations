Brooklyn Nine-Nine

Brooklyn Nine-Nine is an American police live-action sitcom created by Dan Goor and Michael Schur that premiered on Fox. The series revolves around Jake Peralta (Andy Samberg), an immature but talented NYPD detective in Brooklyn's 99th Precinct, who comes into conflict with his new commanding officer, the serious and stern Captain Raymond Holt (Andre Braugher). The ensemble and supporting cast feature Stephanie Beatriz as Rosa Diaz, Terry Crews as Terry Jeffords, Melissa Fumero as Amy Santiago, Joe Lo Truglio as Charles Boyle, Chelsea Peretti as Gina Linetti, Dirk Blocker as Michael Hitchcock, and Joel McKinnon Miller as Norm Scully.

Brooklyn Nine-Nine

TBrooklyn Nine-Nine is complete 24 karat television gold: comedy, action and adventure all wrapped into one. It is a triumph of good writing- the cases that our favorite 99th precinct gets are packed with suspense and hilarious twists. The challenges that the detectives face in their personal lives (especially Charles Boyle) are intelligently written. The characters are all funny, but there is complexity and depth in all of them- they feel real. The chemistry and bond between them is solid too- there is warmth and meaning in all their friendships. The characters may be a bit one-note, but it brings a sense of comfortability, and there is growth over seasons nonetheless. The casting is nothing short of perfect. Andy Samberg is truly a master of his craft- it's impossible not to laugh at his goofy faces and pranks. He is immediately charming and likeable as Jake Peralta.
